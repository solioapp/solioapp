About Solio

What is Solio?

Solio is a next-generation crowdfunding platform built on the Solana blockchain. It allows creators to easily raise funds for their projects and supporters to easily contribute using their crypto wallets.

Why Solana?

  • Speed - Transactions are confirmed within seconds
  • Low Fees - Transaction fees are a fraction of a cent
  • Transparency - All transactions are publicly verifiable on the blockchain
  • Security - Decentralized network ensures the safety of funds

How It Works

1

Create a Project

Register, describe your project, set a goal amount and end date for the fundraiser.

2

Share

Promote your project on social media and attract supporters.

3

Receive Donations

Supporters send SOL directly from their wallets. 100% of funds go to the platform wallet during the fundraiser.

4

Withdraw Funds

After the fundraiser ends, a 2.5% fee is automatically deducted and 97.5% is sent to your wallet.

Fees

Solio charges only 2.5% of the total amount raised. This fee is deducted only at payout, so 100% of donations go to the fundraiser first.

Besides the platform fee, you only pay minimal Solana network transaction fees (typically less than $0.01).

Security

  • Funds are securely stored on the platform wallet during the fundraiser
  • All transactions are verifiable on the Solana blockchain
  • We never have access to your private keys
  • We use standard security protocols to protect data
