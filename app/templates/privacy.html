Privacy Policy

Last updated: {{ now().strftime('%Y-%m-%d') if now is defined else '2024' }}

1. Introduction

This privacy policy explains how Solio collects, uses, and protects your personal data.

2. What Data We Collect

User-provided data:

  • Email and password (for email registration)
  • Username
  • Profile information (bio, photo, social links)
  • Solana wallet address
  • Project data

Automatically collected data:

  • IP address
  • Browser information
  • Date and time of access
  • Blockchain transaction data (public)

3. How We Use Data

  • Providing and improving the service
  • Identity verification and authentication
  • Transaction processing
  • Communication with users
  • Fraud prevention
  • Compliance with legal requirements

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with third parties, except:

  • Service providers (hosting, email)
  • When required by law
  • With your consent

Note: Blockchain transactions are public and visible to everyone.

5. Cookies

We use cookies for:

  • Maintaining login sessions (session cookies)
  • Security (CSRF tokens)
  • Remembering preferences

6. Security

We implement reasonable measures to protect your data:

  • Password encryption (bcrypt)
  • HTTPS communication
  • CSRF attack protection
  • Regular backups

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account
  • Export your data
  • Withdraw consent

8. Data Retention

We retain your data for the duration of your account's existence. After account deletion, personal data is removed, except for data required by law or related to blockchain transactions (which are permanent).

9. Policy Changes

We will notify you of significant changes to this policy via email or announcement on the platform.
